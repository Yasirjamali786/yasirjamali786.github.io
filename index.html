About Me

I began my academic journey at Army Public School & College Dadu, where I completed (SSC) Secondary School Certificate (Matriculation) with A grade from BISE Hyderabad (Board of intermidiate & secondry Education). I further achieved A grade in my (HSSC) Higher Secondary School Certificate (Intermediate) from Degree College Johi. I hold a bachelor's degree in Finance from the University of Sindh, Jamshoro, graduating with a CGPA of 3.20. Beyond my formal education, I have completed a Diploma in Computer Information Technology (CIT), developed a passion for data analysis, and honed my programming skills in Python. I am certified in Google Data Analytics and Google Advanced Data Analytics through Coursera. My interests lie in leveraging data-driven insights and code to solve real-world problems.
