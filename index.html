About Me

Hailing from Dadu, Sindh, I completed my early education at Army Public School and College, where I achieved an A grade from BISE Hyderabad. I further pursued my Intermediate studies at Degree College Johi, also earning an A grade. My academic journey culminated at the University of Sindh, Jamshoro, where I graduated from the Department of Commerce with a specialization in Finance and a CGPA of 3.20.
